# Follow-up Questions for "Deployment Brigade System" Content Brief

## Round 2: Deep Dive Questions

### Metrics & Data
**Q1:** You mentioned the frontend team went from monthly to weekly releases in 2 weeks. What other metrics changed? 
- What was your DORA metrics before/after? (Deployment frequency, lead time, MTTR, change failure rate?)
- How long did deployments take before vs after?
- What happened to incident rates?
- Any data on developer satisfaction or confidence?

### Concrete Day-to-Day Changes
**Q2:** Walk me through what a typical deployment looked like BEFORE the brigade system vs AFTER. 
- Who was in the room/Slack channel?
- What did people say/do?
- How long did it take?
- What happened when something went wrong?

### The Uncomfortable Moment
**Q3:** When you said "I'm rolling this back to match production" and the room went quiet - what happened next? 
- How did people react in the following hours/days?
- Did anyone push back hard? What did they say?
- What surprised you about the response?

### Common Objections
**Q4:** When you've shared this approach with other leaders, what objections do you hear most often?
- "We're too regulated for daily deployments"
- "Our team is too small/large for this structure"
- "We don't have enough automation"
- How do you respond to these?

### Implementation Mistakes
**Q5:** What mistakes did you make when rolling this out? What would you do differently?
- What almost broke?
- What took longer than expected?
- What resistance did you underestimate?
- Any "health code" rules that were too strict or not strict enough?

### Diagnostic Criteria
**Q6:** How should a leader know if their team needs this approach? What are the warning signs?
- Is it just about deployment frequency?
- What about team behaviors/culture?
- What symptoms did you see that screamed "we need this"?

### The "Behind!" Protocol
**Q7:** Give me a real example of the "Behind!" / "Heard!" protocol in action. 
- Exact Slack messages or conversation?
- What environment was being deployed to?
- Did anyone not respond "Heard!" - what happened?

### Rollback Stories
**Q8:** You mentioned "one-button rollback ready" - has anyone actually used it? Tell me about the first time someone hit the rollback button under the new system.
- What failed?
- How fast was the rollback?
- What was the team's response vs what it would have been before?
