About Me

Building teams and systems that scale, with a focus on people and sustainable engineering

My Story

I believe great engineering starts with people. Over the last decade, I've built and scaled teams across the US and Europe, leading initiatives that bridged innovation and culture. At Zendesk, Dutchie, and now Hive, I've helped teams grow sustainably—combining mentorship, clarity, and technical excellence.

My journey in technology began with a deep curiosity about distributed systems and cloud architecture. As I progressed in my career, I discovered that the most impactful work wasn't just about building scalable systems—it was about building scalable teams that could tackle increasingly complex challenges.

Today, I'm passionate about helping engineers grow into leaders, fostering cultures of psychological safety and continuous learning, and architecting systems that balance velocity with reliability. Whether it's reducing cloud costs by 20%, scaling services to handle millions of requests, or mentoring junior engineers into senior roles, I focus on measurable impact that serves both people and business outcomes.

Leadership Values

People First

Great systems are built by great teams. I prioritize psychological safety, clear communication, and creating environments where engineers can do their best work.

Sustainable Velocity

Speed matters, but not at the cost of quality or team health. I believe in building systems and processes that enable consistent, sustainable delivery.

Mentorship & Growth

Every engineer deserves a clear path for growth. I invest time in coaching, feedback, and creating opportunities for team members to develop their skills.

Technical Excellence

Strong engineering fundamentals matter. I advocate for code quality, system design best practices, and making architectural decisions that scale.

Current Focus

At Hive, I'm leading engineering efforts to reinvent distributed cloud infrastructure. We're building reliable, affordable storage and compute solutions that leverage decentralized technology. It's an exciting challenge that combines cutting-edge distributed systems with practical business needs.

Beyond my day-to-day work, I'm passionate about:

  • Speaking & Teaching: Sharing lessons learned about distributed teams, cloud architecture, and engineering leadership
  • Mentorship: Helping engineers navigate career transitions and develop leadership skills
  • Community Building: Contributing to engineering culture conversations and best practices

Beyond Engineering

When I'm not building teams or systems, you'll find me exploring Sardinia's beautiful coastline, experimenting with new technologies, or enjoying good food and wine with family. I'm based in Cagliari, Italy, but work with distributed teams across multiple time zones—proof that great collaboration knows no borders.
